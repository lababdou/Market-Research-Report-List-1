<p><p>The mobile toilet market consists of two main types: handling type and trailer type. Handling type mobile toilets are smaller, portable units that can be easily moved by hand. They are commonly used in outdoor events or construction sites. On the other hand, trailer type mobile toilets are larger units that are towed by a vehicle. They usually have multiple compartments and are often used in more permanent locations such as campsites or parks. Both types provide convenient and hygienic options for temporary or remote sanitation needs.</p></p>
