Turning machines rotate the workpiece to create cylindrical components. Grinding machines use an abrasive wheel to remove material from a workpiece's surface. Electrical discharge machines use electrical sparks to erode materials and create intricate shapes. These different types of machines cater to specific manufacturing needs, ensuring efficiency and accuracy in the production process.</p></p>
