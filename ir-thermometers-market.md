<p><strong>The IR Thermometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ear</li><li>Forehead</li><li>Multifunction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IR Thermometers Market comprises three main types: ear thermometers, forehead thermometers, and multifunction thermometers. Ear thermometers are specifically designed to measure body temperature by measuring the infrared heat emitted by the eardrum. Forehead thermometers, on the other hand, measure body temperature by scanning the temporal artery located on the forehead. Multifunction thermometers offer the flexibility to measure body temperature through both ear and forehead methods, providing users with various options. These different types cater to the diverse preferences and needs of consumers in measuring body temperature accurately and conveniently.</p></p>
