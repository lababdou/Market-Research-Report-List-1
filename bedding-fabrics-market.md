<p><strong>The Bedding Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven Fabric</li><li>Knitted Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Woven fabric is a type of bedding fabric that is made by interlacing two sets of yarns together at right angles, creating a strong and durable material. It is commonly used in bedding products such as sheets and pillowcases. On the other hand, knitted fabric is created by interlooping a single yarn, resulting in a stretchy and breathable material. This type of fabric is often used in bedding products like blankets and mattress covers. Both woven and knitted fabrics serve different purposes in the bedding industry, catering to various consumer preferences.</p></p>
