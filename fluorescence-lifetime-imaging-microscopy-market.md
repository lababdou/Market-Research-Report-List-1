<p><strong>The Fluorescence Lifetime Imaging Microscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time-Domain</li><li>Frequency-Domain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescence Lifetime Imaging Microscopy (FLIM) is a technique used in biomedical research and imaging. In the Time-Domain market, FLIM measures the time delay between a laser pulse and the emission of fluorescent light, providing information about the lifetime of fluophores. On the other hand, the Frequency-Domain market uses modulated excitation light, which helps extract phase and demodulation ratio for lifetime calculations. Both methods offer advantages and limitations, enabling researchers to study various biological samples and processes with high precision and accuracy.</p></p>
