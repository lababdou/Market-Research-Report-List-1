<p><p>Drain cleaning equipment market comprises two main types: hand tools and power tools. Hand tools include basic equipment like plungers, drain snakes, and augers that are manually operated by individuals. These tools are commonly used for minor blockages or small drains. On the other hand, power tools are electrically or battery-powered devices that offer more efficiency and effectiveness in clearing stubborn clogs. These tools include high-pressure water jetters, sewer cameras, and electric drain cleaners, which are suitable for larger drains or complex blockage issues.</p></p>
