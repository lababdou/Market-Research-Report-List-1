<p><strong>The Traditional Probe Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blade/Tungsten Probe Cards</li><li>Epoxy/Cantilever Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traditional probe cards are used in semiconductor testing to make electrical connections between the test equipment and the semiconductor devices. There are two main types: blade/tungsten probe cards and epoxy/cantilever probe cards. Blade/tungsten probe cards use sharp, durable blades to make contact with the device under test, allowing for high-density probing. Epoxy/cantilever probe cards use flexible cantilevers coated with conductive epoxy, providing better contact accuracy and lower probe marks on the device. Both types have their advantages and are used in different applications based on specific testing requirements.</p></p>
