<p><p>Coast Surveillance Radar is used to monitor and track maritime activities along coastlines. The market for Coast Surveillance Radar can be categorized into three types: X-Band Radar, S-Band Radar, and Others. X-Band Radar uses a frequency range between 8-12 GHz and provides high resolution and accuracy, making it suitable for short-range surveillance. S-Band Radar operates at a frequency range between 2-4 GHz, performing medium to long-range surveillance tasks. The remaining "Others" category includes radars that do not fall into the X-Band or S-Band frequency ranges and may offer unique features or capabilities.</p></p>
