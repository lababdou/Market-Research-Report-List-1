<p><p>The ring sorter market includes three types: toroidal cross belt, bag sorter, and tilting plate sorter. Toroidal cross belt sorters consist of a looped conveyor belt that moves items in a circular path for sorting. Bag sorters use bags or pouches to carry items through the sorting process. Tilting plate sorters use a series of plates that tilt to redirect items into appropriate chutes. These sorting technologies are used in various industries such as logistics, e-commerce, and postal services to efficiently sort and distribute items based on predetermined criteria.</p></p>
