<p><p>Ligament prostheses are used to replace damaged ligaments in the body. There are two types of ligament prostheses market: allograft prostheses and synthetic prostheses. Allograft prostheses are made from human tissues obtained from a donor, which are processed and sterilized before implantation. Both types aim to provide stability and function to the injured area, but the choice depends on factors like availability, durability, and patient preference.</p></p>
