<p><p>Industrial vacuum distillation units are used to separate and purify liquids by utilizing a vacuum to lower the boiling point of the substances. There are two main types of these units: dry type and wet type. </p><p>Dry type units are designed to remove liquids without the need for water or any other liquid. They rely on a powerful vacuum system to evaporate and separate the substances, making them suitable for applications where water cannot be used or would be problematic.</p><p>Wet type units, on the other hand, involve the use of liquid to aid the separation process. Water or another specific liquid is introduced into the system to create a liquid-vapor mixture, enhancing the separation efficiency. Wet type units are commonly used in applications where liquids need to be recovered or recycled.</p></p>
