<p><p>Forensic swabs are essential tools used in crime scene investigations for collecting and preserving valuable evidence. There are three main types of swabs available in the market: foam, flocked, and cotton. Foam swabs have a spongy material that effectively picks up and retains biological fluids and particulates. Flocked swabs have a soft brush-like tip that provides excellent sample collection and elution, making it ideal for DNA analysis. Cotton swabs have been traditionally used in forensic practices for their versatility and low cost. Each type offers unique advantages and can be chosen based on specific requirements in the forensic field.</p></p>
