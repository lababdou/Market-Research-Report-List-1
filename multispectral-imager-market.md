<p><p>The multispectral imager market offers three different types of imaging systems: multi-lens type, multi-camera type, and beam split type. In the multi-lens type, multiple lenses are used in a single imaging system to capture different wavelengths simultaneously. The multi-camera type employs multiple cameras, each configured to capture a specific wavelength range. Lastly, the beam split type divides the incoming light into different channels using beam splitters, allowing for the detection of various wavelengths. These imaging systems cater to different application needs in industries such as agriculture, environmental monitoring, and healthcare.</p></p>
