Ultrasonic radar chips employ sound waves to measure distances and recognize obstacles, commonly found in parking sensors and robotics. Lidar chips depend on laser technology to create detailed 3D maps and enable accurate object detection, vital for autonomous vehicles and drones. These diverse market segments showcase the continuous innovation and demand for radar chip solutions in various industries.</p></p>
