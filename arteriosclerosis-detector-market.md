<p><p>The Arteriosclerosis Detector market is divided into two types: Pulse Wave Velocity Detection and Pulse Waveform Analyzer Detection. Pulse Wave Velocity Detection involves measuring the speed of pulse waves to assess the stiffness of arteries, which helps diagnose arteriosclerosis. Pulse Waveform Analyzer Detection, on the other hand, analyzes the shape and characteristics of pulse waveforms to determine arterial health. Both methods are used to detect and monitor arteriosclerosis, a condition that causes the hardening and narrowing of arteries, leading to cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
