<p><ul><li>Self-Erecting Tower Cranes</li><li>Flat Top Tower Cranes</li><li>Hammerhead Tower Cranes</li><li>Luffing Jib Tower Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The used tower cranes market consists of various types of cranes that are available for resale. Self-erecting tower cranes are compact and easy to set up, making them ideal for smaller construction sites. Flat top tower cranes have a flat perch on top and are suitable for projects that require high lifting capacities. Hammerhead tower cranes have a horizontal jib with a counterweight and are commonly used in high-rise construction. Luffing jib tower cranes have a flexible jib that can be angled vertically, making them well-suited for congested sites or where multiple cranes are required.</p></p>
