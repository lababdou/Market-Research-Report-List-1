<p><p>The Carbon Dioxide (CO2) laser market can be classified into three types: Continuous Output, Pulse Output, and Q-Switch Output. Continuous Output lasers emit a continuous beam of laser energy, which is suitable for cutting and welding applications. Pulse Output lasers emit short bursts of energy, making them ideal for drilling and marking purposes. Q-Switch Output lasers produce high-intensity pulses in a short time span, making them suitable for precise cutting and engraving tasks. These different market types cater to various industrial applications, offering versatility and efficiency in laser processing.</p></p>
