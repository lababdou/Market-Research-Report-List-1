<p><p>In the 5G base station market, there are two main types: macro and small base stations. Macro base stations are larger in size and have a wider coverage area, typically used in urban and suburban areas. These base stations are built on tall buildings or towers. On the other hand, small base stations are smaller and compact, used in high-density areas like shopping malls or stadiums to provide localized connectivity and enhance network capacity. They are usually installed on street poles or rooftops. Both types of base stations play a crucial role in ensuring seamless 5G network connectivity.</p></p>
