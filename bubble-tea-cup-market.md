<p><p>Bubble tea, also known as boba tea or pearl milk tea, is a popular Taiwanese tea-based drink that originated in the 1980s. The drink is traditionally served with a wide straw to allow consumers to enjoy the chewy pearls or jellies.</p><p>A bubble tea cup is specifically designed to hold and serve bubble tea. These cups are typically made of plastic or paper and come in a variety of sizes to accommodate different serving portions. They often feature a screw-on lid with a small opening for the straw.</p><p>The bubble tea cup market has experienced significant growth in recent years. The increasing popularity of bubble tea among consumers, especially the younger demographic, has been a key driver of market growth. Additionally, the increasing number of bubble tea chains and shops across the globe has boosted the demand for bubble tea cups.</p><p>Moreover, the growing preference for eco-friendly and sustainable packaging solutions has led to the introduction of biodegradable cups made from materials like paper and PLA (polylactic acid) in the market.
