<p><ul><li>Single Chamber</li><li>Multi Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscope washer market can be categorized into two types: single chamber and multi chamber. Single chamber endoscope washers consist of a single chamber where the endoscope is inserted for washing. They are compact and suitable for smaller healthcare facilities. On the other hand, multi chamber endoscope washers have multiple chambers that can accommodate multiple endoscopes simultaneously. They are larger and more suitable for bigger healthcare facilities with higher volumes of endoscope usage.</p></p>
