<p><p>The Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring (ABPM) Devices market consists of two main types: Ordinary ABPM and Mobile-based ABPM. </p><p>Ordinary ABPM devices are portable devices that measure blood pressure at regular intervals over a 24-hour period. These devices are typically worn on the arm and can provide accurate readings for diagnosis and monitoring of hypertension.</p><p>Mobile-based ABPM devices, on the other hand, utilize smartphones or wearable devices to measure blood pressure. These devices offer convenience and mobility, allowing users to monitor their blood pressure anytime, anywhere. They often incorporate advanced technology and connectivity features to provide real-time data and analysis.</p></p>
