<p><p>An insulated box, also known as a thermal box or cold box, is a specialized container used for storing and transporting temperature-sensitive products. The increasing demand for temperature-controlled packaging solutions for perishable goods is driving the market growth. With the growth of e-commerce and the global supply chain, there is a rising need for effective and reliable temperature-sensitive packaging. The insulated box market is witnessing a substantial increase in demand from industries such as pharmaceuticals and food and beverages, where maintaining product integrity and quality are crucial.</p><p>The current market for insulated boxes is growing at a significant rate. The market is driven by factors such as the increasing awareness of the benefits of temperature-controlled packaging, stringent regulations for transporting temperature-sensitive products, and the expansion of the cold chain logistics industry.
