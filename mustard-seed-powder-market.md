<p><p>Mustard seed powder is available in three main market types: yellow mustard flour, brown mustard flour, and golden mustard flour. Yellow mustard flour is made from yellow mustard seeds and has a mild and tangy flavor. Brown mustard flour is derived from brown mustard seeds and has a stronger and spicier taste. Golden mustard flour is made from a combination of both yellow and brown mustard seeds, resulting in a balanced and flavorful powder. These different market types offer varying flavors and can be used in a variety of culinary applications.</p></p>
