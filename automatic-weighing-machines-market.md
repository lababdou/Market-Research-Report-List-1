<p><strong>The Automatic Weighing Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Weighing Machines Market consists of two main types: In-Motion Checkweighers and Intermittent Checkweighers. In-Motion Checkweighers are designed to weigh objects while they are in motion, typically used in industries with high-speed production lines. They ensure accurate weight measurements and reject any under or overweight items. On the other hand, Intermittent Checkweighers are used to weigh static objects, often found in industries with slower-paced production lines. These machines provide accurate weight readings and are suitable for applications where objects need to be individually weighed.</p></p>
