<p><p>Actuators are mechanical devices that convert different forms of energy into motion. The market for actuators can be broadly categorized into three types: pneumatic actuators, hydraulic actuators, and electric actuators. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to generate motion, hydraulic actuators use fluid pressure, and electric actuators use electricity to produce movement. Additionally, there are other types of actuators available in the market, such as mechanical actuators and thermal actuators, which utilize mechanical and thermal energy respectively to generate motion. These various actuator types serve different applications and industries based on their specific operational requirements.</p></p>
