<p><strong>The Perilla Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toasted Perilla Oil</li><li>Untoasted Perilla Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perilla oil comes in two main market types: toasted perilla oil and untoasted perilla oil. Toasted perilla oil is made by roasting the seeds before extracting the oil, resulting in a nutty and robust flavor. It is commonly used in Asian cuisines for its unique taste and aroma. On the other hand, untoasted perilla oil is made without roasting the seeds, giving it a lighter and milder flavor. This type of oil is often preferred for dressings, marinades, and dishes that require a more subtle taste. Both variations offer distinct options for culinary applications.</p></p>
