<p><p>The household solar panels market consists of two main types: monocrystalline solar panels and polycrystalline solar panels. Monocrystalline solar panels are made from a single crystal structure, resulting in higher efficiency and sleeker appearance, but at a higher cost. Polycrystalline solar panels, on the other hand, are made from multiple crystal structures, offering a lower cost but slightly lower efficiency. Both types convert sunlight into electricity and are increasingly popular for generating clean and renewable energy in households.</p></p>
