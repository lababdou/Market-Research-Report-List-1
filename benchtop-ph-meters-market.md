<p><p>The benchtop pH meters market includes three types: single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel meters. Single channel pH meters have only one input channel, allowing the measurement of pH in a single sample at a time. Dual channel pH meters have two input channels, enabling the simultaneous measurement of pH in two separate samples. Multi-channel pH meters have multiple input channels, which can measure pH in multiple samples simultaneously. These different types cater to the varying needs of end-users, offering flexibility and efficiency in pH measurement and analysis.</p></p>
