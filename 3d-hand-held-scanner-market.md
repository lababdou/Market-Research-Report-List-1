<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D hand-held scanner market consists of two main types: laser and structured light. Laser scanners use laser beams to capture precise measurements of an object's surface, allowing for high accuracy and detailed scans. On the other hand, structured light scanners project patterns of light onto the object and analyze the distortion to generate a 3D model. These scanners offer faster scanning speeds and can capture color information as well. Both types have their advantages and are widely used in industries like manufacturing, healthcare, and entertainment.</p></p>
