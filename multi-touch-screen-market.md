<p><strong>The Multi-Touch Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Technology</li><li>Resistive Technology</li><li>Acoustic Technology</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-touch screen market is divided into various types based on the technology used. Capacitive technology screens are the most common, using the electrical charge of the human body to detect touch. Resistive technology screens use pressure on two conductive layers to detect touch. Acoustic technology screens use sound waves to detect touch. There are also other types of screens available in the market that use different technologies like infrared, optical imaging, and electric field sensing. Each technology offers different features and advantages for users to interact with touch screens.</p></p>
