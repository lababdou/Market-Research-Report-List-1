<p><p>Galvos, or galvanometers, are devices used in various fields for precise control and positioning of laser beams. They come in different market types: single axis, dual axis, and multi-axis. Single-axis galvos can move the laser beam along one plane, while dual-axis galvos enable movement along two planes, resulting in more complex patterns. Multi-axis galvos add another level of freedom, allowing for movement along multiple planes simultaneously. These market types cater to different applications and user requirements, offering increasing degrees of flexibility and control over laser beam positioning.</p></p>
