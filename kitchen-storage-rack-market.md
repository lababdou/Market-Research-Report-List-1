<p><p>The kitchen storage rack market offers three types of racks: floor-standing, wall-mounted, and desktop racks. Floor-standing racks are designed to stand on the kitchen floor and are usually bigger in size, providing ample storage space. Wall-mounted racks are installed on walls, saving valuable floor space and offering a convenient solution for organizing utensils and supplies. Desktop racks are smaller in size and are meant to be placed on countertops or tables, providing easy access to frequently used items. These different types cater to various kitchen layouts and storage needs.</p></p>
