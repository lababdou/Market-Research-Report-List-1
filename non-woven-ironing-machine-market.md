<p><p>The non-woven ironing machine market can be categorized into three types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and others. Fully automatic machines are equipped with advanced technology and allow for automatic feeding, ironing, and folding of non-woven fabrics, making them highly efficient and time-saving. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention for tasks such as feeding the fabrics, but they still offer automated ironing and folding functions. The "others" category encompasses machines that may have unique features or functionalities not covered by the fully automatic or semi-automatic types.</p></p>
