<p><p>The micro mobility telematic device market can be classified into two types: embedded and integrated. </p><p>Embedded devices are typically built into the micro mobility vehicle during the manufacturing process. They are seamlessly integrated with the vehicle's system, providing real-time data about location, speed, and other relevant information. </p><p>On the other hand, integrated devices are separate hardware units that can be attached or mounted onto the micro mobility vehicle. These devices are designed to work in conjunction with the vehicle's existing systems, enabling data transmission and connectivity for better tracking and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
