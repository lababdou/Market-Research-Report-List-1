<p><p>The Quarter-Turn Valve market encompasses various types of valves, including Ball Type, Globe Type, Plug Type, and others. Ball Type valves are characterized by a floating ball inside the valve body that controls the flow by rotating a quarter turn. Globe Type valves have a globe-shaped valve seat and a movable disc that regulates the flow. Plug Type valves have a cylindrical or tapered plug that rotates within the valve body to control the flow. Other types of valves in this market include Butterfly Valves, Gate Valves, and Check Valves, among others. These valves serve different purposes but all function by rotating a quarter turn to manage the flow of fluids.</p></p>
