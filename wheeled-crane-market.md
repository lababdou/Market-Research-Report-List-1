<p><p>The wheeled crane market consists of two main types: cross-country crane and all-road crane. Cross-country cranes are designed for off-road terrains and are equipped with features like all-wheel drive and enhanced ground clearance, allowing them to navigate through rough and uneven surfaces. On the other hand, all-road cranes are versatile machines capable of both on-road and off-road operations. They are equipped with features like telescoping booms, hydraulic jacks, and stabilizers, enabling them to perform a wide range of lifting tasks in various terrains and conditions.</p></p>
