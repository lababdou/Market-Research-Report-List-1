<p><p>Reduced Pressure Zone Devices (RPZDs) are mechanical backflow prevention devices that protect potable water supplies from contamination. RPZDs are typically used in commercial and industrial buildings, as well as in certain residential applications where there is a higher risk of contamination.</p><p>The global market for RPZDs has been experiencing steady growth over the years. The increasing awareness about the importance of maintaining water quality and preventing waterborne diseases has been a key factor driving the market growth. Additionally, stringent government regulations and guidelines regarding backflow prevention in various industries have also contributed to the market demand.</p><p>The market is expected to witness further growth in the coming years.
