<p><p>The proteomics instrument market consists of various types, including Mass Spectrometry, Liquid Chromatography, Electrophoresis, and Others. Mass Spectrometry is a technique that measures the mass and abundance of molecules, providing qualitative and quantitative data. Liquid Chromatography separates and purifies complex mixtures of proteins based on their affinity to the stationary phase. Electrophoresis separates proteins based on their charge and size using an electric field. Other techniques in the market include protein microarrays, protein fractionation systems, and protein synthesis systems, which aid in various proteomic analyses.</p></p>
