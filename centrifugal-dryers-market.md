<p><p>Centrifugal dryers are a type of industrial drying equipment that use centrifugal force to remove moisture from materials. There are three main types: hot air centrifugal dryers, high-speed centrifugal spray dryers, and industrial centrifugal dryers. Hot air centrifugal dryers use heated air to dry materials, while high-speed centrifugal spray dryers use a high-speed rotating disc to atomize the liquid and quickly dry it. Industrial centrifugal dryers are larger in size and designed for heavy-duty drying applications in industries such as food processing and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
