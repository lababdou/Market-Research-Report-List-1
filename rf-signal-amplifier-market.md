<p><p>RF signal amplifiers are electronic devices used to boost weak radio frequency signals for better transmission. In the digital market, amplifiers are designed specifically for processing digital RF signals, which are encoded into discrete numerical values. These amplifiers focus on ensuring accurate and error-free signal amplification, commonly used in digital communication systems. On the other hand, analog amplifiers work with continuous RF signals where the amplitude and frequency vary smoothly. These amplifiers are commonly used in analog communication systems, emphasizing faithful reproduction of the input signal for clearer communication.</p></p>
