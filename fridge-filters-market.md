<p><strong>The Fridge Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters</li><li>Carbon Block Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fridge Filters market consists of two main types: Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters and Carbon Block Filters. GAC Filters use a small amount of activated carbon granules to remove impurities and odors from the water. On the other hand, Carbon Block Filters are made of compressed carbon particles, which offer a larger surface area for better filtration. Both types effectively eliminate contaminants and improve the taste and quality of water in fridges. However, Carbon Block Filters are generally considered more efficient due to their finer filtration capability.</p></p>
