<p><p>The 3-piece ball valves market includes three types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual ball valves are operated by hand, requiring physical movement to control the flow. Pneumatic ball valves use compressed air or gas to automate their function, allowing for remote control and quick response. Electric ball valves, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and offer the advantages of automated operation and precise control. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, providing a range of options for customers in the market.</p></p>
