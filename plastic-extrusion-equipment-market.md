<p><strong>The Plastic Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blown Film Extrusion Equipment</li><li>Sheet Extrusion Equipment</li><li>Pipe Extrusion Equipment</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plastic Extrusion Equipment market is categorized into four main types. Blown Film Extrusion Equipment is used to create plastic films by melting and extruding plastic materials through a circular die. Sheet Extrusion Equipment produces plastic sheets by melting plastic materials and forcing them through a die. Pipe Extrusion Equipment is used to manufacture plastic pipes by melting plastic materials and shaping them using a die. Lastly, the Others category includes various types of extrusion equipment used for manufacturing plastic products such as profiles, rods, tubes, and cables.</p></p>
