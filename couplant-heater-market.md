<p><strong>The Couplant Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Cup</li><li>Double Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Couplant heaters are devices used to keep the couplant material warm during ultrasonic testing. Single cup couplant heaters consist of a single cup that can hold the couplant material and keeps it warm using a heating element. They are compact and portable. On the other hand, double cup couplant heaters have two cups, one for the couplant material and the other for the heating element. They offer the advantage of heating the couplant material more evenly and maintaining its temperature for longer durations. Double cup couplant heaters are generally larger and less portable than single cup heaters.</p></p>
