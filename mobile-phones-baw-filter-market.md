<p><strong>The Mobile Phones BAW Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FBAR BAW Filter</li><li>SMR BAW Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile phones BAW filter market encompasses two major types, namely FBAR BAW filter and SMR BAW filter. FBAR BAW filter, or Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator BAW filter, is a device that employs a thin film to support an acoustic resonator for filtering signals in mobile phones. On the other hand, SMR BAW filter, or Solidly Mounted Resonator BAW filter, utilizes a solidly mounted resonator instead of a thin film. Both types of BAW filters play a crucial role in enhancing mobile phone performance by filtering unwanted signals and improving overall signal quality.</p></p>
