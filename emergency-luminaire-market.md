<p><p>The emergency luminaire market includes three major types: LED, fluorescent, and incandescent. LED emergency luminaires utilize light-emitting diodes to produce bright illumination with low energy consumption. These luminaires are highly efficient and have a long lifespan. Fluorescent emergency luminaires employ fluorescent lamps that create light through the excitation of phosphors, providing high brightness and energy efficiency. However, they have a shorter lifespan compared to LEDs. Incandescent emergency luminaires use traditional incandescent bulbs, which generate light by heating a filament. Although these luminaires have a shorter lifespan and consume more energy, they are still used in certain applications.</p></p>
