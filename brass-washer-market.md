<p><p>A brass washer is a type of washer made from brass material, which is an alloy of copper and zinc. It is commonly used in plumbing, electrical, and automotive industries for various applications. Brass washers are highly resistant to corrosion, have good electrical conductivity, and provide a tight seal when pressed between two surfaces. These qualities make them suitable for use in environments where moisture and electrical conductivity are concerns.</p><p>Market analysis suggests that the brass washer market is expected to witness steady growth in the forecast period.
