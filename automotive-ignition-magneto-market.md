<p><p>The automotive ignition magneto market includes two main types of systems: self-contained systems and battery-powered systems. Self-contained systems generate electricity using permanent magnets and do not require an external power source. On the other hand, battery-powered systems rely on a battery to generate electricity for ignition. Self-contained systems are typically found in smaller engines like lawnmowers and chainsaws, while battery-powered systems are more common in larger vehicles like cars and motorcycles. Each type of system offers its own advantages and is suited to different applications.</p></p>
