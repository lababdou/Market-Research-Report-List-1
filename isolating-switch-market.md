<p><p>The isolating switch market can be categorized into two main types: high voltage isolating switch and low voltage isolating switch. On the other hand, a low voltage isolating switch is used in low voltage electrical systems, typically below 1kV, and provides the same function of safely isolating circuits but at lower voltage levels. Both types of isolating switches play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of electrical systems during maintenance or repair activities.</p></p>
