<p><p>The Cadmium Telluride Photovoltaic market consists of three main types: Single Crystals, Large Grains, and Standard Cells. Single Crystals refer to the high-purity and highly crystalline form of Cadmium Telluride, which offers superior efficiency but is expensive to produce. Large Grains contain larger crystal structures and are less expensive but also less efficient compared to Single Crystals. Standard Cells are the most common type, made of smaller grains and offering moderate efficiency at a lower cost. Each type caters to different market segments with varying cost and efficiency priorities.</p></p>
