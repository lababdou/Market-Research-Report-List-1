<p><p>LC-MS (liquid chromatography-mass spectrometry) software market can be broadly classified into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. It allows users to access and analyze their LC-MS data from anywhere and at any time. On the other hand, on-premise LC-MS software is installed and run locally on the user's own hardware infrastructure.
