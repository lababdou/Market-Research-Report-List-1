<p><p>Alarm sounders are devices that emit loud audible alerts in case of an emergency or danger. There are primarily three types of alarm sounders available in the market. The first type is wall-mounting sounders, which are fixed on walls or any vertical surfaces. The second type is self-standing sounders, which are conveniently placed on flat surfaces without the need for additional support. The third type includes other variations such as ceiling-mounted sounders or sounder beacons, which provide both audible and visual warnings. These different types cater to diverse installation requirements and ensure effective alerting in emergency situations.</p></p>
