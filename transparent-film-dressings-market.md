<p><p>Transparent film dressings are a type of medical dressing used for wound care. There are two main categories: transparent wound dressings and transparent self-adhesive dressings. Transparent wound dressings are made of a thin, clear film that allows easy visualization of the wound while protecting it from bacteria and moisture. They are typically used for shallow wounds with minimal exudate. On the other hand, transparent self-adhesive dressings have an adhesive border that helps secure the dressing in place, making them suitable for wounds that require better adherence and protection.</p></p>
