<p><p>Switched Mode Power Supplies (SMPS) are electronic devices that convert electrical power efficiently. The market includes several types of SMPS. DC-DC converters transfer power from one DC source to another at different voltage levels. Forward converters convert electrical energy from input to output in a one-way flow. Flyback converters store energy in a magnetic field and transfer it to the output. Self-oscillating flyback converters use a self-oscillating circuit to control the energy transfer. All these types are crucial components in various applications that require efficient power conversion.</p></p>
