<p><p>The COVID-19 testing devices market consists of three types: nucleic acid testing devices, antigen testing devices, and serological testing devices. Nucleic acid testing devices detect the presence of the virus's genetic material, providing accurate results but requiring laboratory analysis. Antigen testing devices detect specific proteins on the virus's surface and deliver rapid results, making them suitable for point-of-care testing. Serological testing devices identify antibodies produced by the body in response to the virus, indicating prior infection. Each type has its advantages and applications in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19.</p></p>
