<p><p>Twin screw extruders are commonly used in various industries for homogeneous mixing and processing of materials. The market for these extruders can be divided into three types: parallel co-rotating TSE, parallel counter-rotating TSE, and conical twin screw extruders. Parallel co-rotating TSE has two screws rotating in the same direction, providing high shear and mixing properties. Parallel counter-rotating TSE features two screws rotating in opposite directions for better conveying and compounding capabilities. Conical twin screw extruders have tapered screws, enabling efficient conveying and melting of materials.</p></p>
