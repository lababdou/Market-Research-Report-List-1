<p><strong>The dPCR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Droplet Digital PCR</li><li>Chip-based Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dPCR (digital PCR) market consists of various types, namely Droplet Digital PCR (ddPCR) and Chip-based Digital PCR. ddPCR is a technique that partitions a DNA sample into thousands of droplets and quantifies the target DNA molecules within each droplet, providing a highly accurate and precise measurement of nucleic acid levels. Chip-based Digital PCR employs a microfluidic chip to divide the DNA sample into multiple reaction chambers, allowing for simultaneous amplification and quantification of the target DNA. Both techniques offer sensitive and quantitative analysis for a variety of applications in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
