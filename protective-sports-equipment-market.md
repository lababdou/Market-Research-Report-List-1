<p><p>Protective sports equipment can be divided into different types based on the body part it primarily aims to protect. Head and face equipment include helmets and face shields, designed to safeguard against head and facial injuries. Trunk and thorax equipment, such as chest protectors or rib guards, protect vital organs like the heart and lungs. Upper extremity equipment includes items like shoulder pads and elbow guards, which shield against injuries to the arms and shoulders. Lower extremity equipment, such as shin guards or knee pads, provides protection for the legs and knees during sports activities.</p></p>
