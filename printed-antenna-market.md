<p><strong>The Printed Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ink-jet Printing</li><li>Screen Printing</li><li>Flexography Printing</li><li>Gravure Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printed antenna market includes various printing technologies used to manufacture antennas. Ink-jet printing involves the deposition of conductive ink droplets onto a substrate, while screen printing makes use of a fine mesh stencil to transfer ink onto the substrate. Flexography printing utilizes flexible relief plates to print conductive patterns, and gravure printing employs engraved cylinders to print conductive ink onto the substrate. These printing techniques allow for the production of antennas with unique designs, shapes, and sizes, catering to different application requirements in wireless communication systems.</p></p>
