<p><p>The Crystal Growing Furnace market consists of two main types: Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace and Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace. The CZ Method Furnace is widely used in semiconductor manufacturing, where a seed crystal is dipped into a crucible filled with molten material, then slowly pulled and rotated to form a single crystal. On the other hand, the FZ Method Furnace uses a high-frequency induction heating system to melt and solidify a sample, creating a single crystal. Both methods play a vital role in the production of high-quality crystals used in various industries.</p></p>
