<p><p>A brain retractor is a surgical instrument used to hold back brain tissues during neurosurgery procedures. Brain retractors come in various designs and sizes, providing flexibility and precision to the surgeons.</p><p>The global brain retractor market is expected to witness steady growth in the forecast period. The development of innovative brain retractor systems with improved functionality, such as adjustable arms and retraction blades, is enhancing surgical outcomes. <p><p>The Brain Retractor market is segmented into three types: Cerebrum Retractors, Cerebellar Retractors, and Brain Stem Retractors. Cerebrum Retractors are specifically designed to retract and hold the cerebrum during brain surgeries. Cerebellar Retractors are used to retract and hold the cerebellum during procedures involving the posterior fossa of the brain. Brain Stem Retractors are utilized to retract and hold the brain stem during surgeries involving this critical area. Each type of retractor serves a specific purpose in ensuring precise surgical access and minimizing tissue damage during brain surgeries.</p></p>
