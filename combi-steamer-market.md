<p><p>The Combi Steamer market is categorized into gas type and electric type. Gas type combi steamers operate using natural gas or propane as a fuel source, providing high heat and faster cooking times. They are commonly used in commercial kitchens where gas connections are available. Electric type combi steamers, on the other hand, run on electricity and are preferred where gas supply is limited or absent. They offer precise temperature control and are energy-efficient. Both types of combi steamers serve the same purpose of providing versatile cooking methods by combining steam, heat, and convection.</p></p>
