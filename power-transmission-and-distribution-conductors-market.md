<p><p>Power transmission and distribution conductors market consists of different types of conductors such as AAC (all aluminum conductor) and ACSR (aluminum conductor steel reinforced). AAC conductors are lightweight and have excellent corrosion resistance, making them ideal for use in urban areas. On the other hand, ACSR conductors are known for their high tensile strength due to the steel reinforcement, making them suitable for longer distance transmission lines. Both types of conductors play a crucial role in the efficient and reliable distribution of electricity across various infrastructure projects.</p></p>
