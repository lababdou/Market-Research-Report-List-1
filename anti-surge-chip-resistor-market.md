<p><p>The anti-surge chip resistor market is categorized into three types: thick film resistors, thin film resistors, and others. Thick film resistors are made by depositing a thick layer of resistive material on a ceramic substrate, providing high power handling capabilities and low cost. Thin film resistors, on the other hand, are created by depositing a thin layer of resistive material on a substrate, offering superior stability, accuracy, and precision. The "others" category includes various types of resistors such as carbon film resistors, metal film resistors, and wirewound resistors, each with their own unique features and applications within the anti-surge chip resistor market.</p></p>
