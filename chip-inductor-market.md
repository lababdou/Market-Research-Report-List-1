<p><strong>The Chip Inductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Winding Type</li><li>Laminated Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Weaving Type</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chip inductor market is segmented into various types based on their construction. Winding type chip inductors have a coil wound around a magnetic core, providing high inductance values. Laminated type chip inductors have multiple layers of ferrite material, allowing for compact size and low DC resistance. Film type chip inductors use thin film technology, offering high-frequency performance and high quality factors. Weaving type chip inductors have windings and magnetic cores intertwined, providing a wide range of inductance values. Other types include multilayer chip inductors, which offer high inductance in a multilayer structure.</p></p>
