<p><p>Radiation sterilization equipment is divided into three types: E-Beam Radiation Sterilization, X-Ray Sterilization, and Gamma Sterilization. E-Beam radiation sterilization involves using high-energy electrons to eliminate microorganisms and achieve sterilization. X-Ray sterilization employs high-energy X-rays to destroy bacteria and other pathogens. Gamma sterilization employs ionizing gamma radiation from a radioactive isotope source. Each type of equipment has its own advantages and applications in the sterilization industry.</p></p>
