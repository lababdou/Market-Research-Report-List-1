<p><p>Single Domain Antibody Platforms Market Types refer to different forms or variations of single domain antibodies (SDAs) that are being developed and marketed. Multivalent Single-Domain Antibodies have multiple binding sites for the same target, enhancing their binding strength and efficacy. Mono-specific Single-Domain Antibodies are SDAs that specifically bind to a single target, making them useful in targeting specific diseases or proteins.</p></p>
