<p><p>The plastic bearing market is divided into two main types: plastic rolling bearings and plastic sliding bearings. Plastic rolling bearings are designed with rolling elements, such as balls or rollers, to facilitate smooth motion and reduce friction. They are commonly used in applications that require high-speed rotation, such as in machinery and equipment. On the other hand, plastic sliding bearings are designed to allow sliding motion between two surfaces, providing low-friction performance. They are commonly used in applications where lubrication may be limited or unavailable, such as medical devices and food processing equipment.</p></p>
