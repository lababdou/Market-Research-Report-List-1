<p><p>In the mobile device market, IC substrates play a crucial role in connecting integrated circuits to the main circuit board. There are several types of IC substrates available. WB BGA Substrate refers to wire bonding ball grid array substrate, which uses wire bonding technology for interconnections. WB CSP Substrate stands for chip scale packaging substrate, which is a compact version of the BGA substrate. FC BGA Substrate means flip chip ball grid array substrate, where the integrated circuits are directly connected using solder bumps. FC CSP Substrate is the flip chip chip scale packaging substrate. Lastly, there are other types of IC substrates available in the market as well.</p></p>
