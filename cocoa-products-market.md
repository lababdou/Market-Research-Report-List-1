<p><p>The cocoa products market can be categorized into four types: cocoa powder, cocoa paste, cocoa butter, and cocoa beans. Cocoa powder is made by extracting cocoa solids from cocoa beans and is commonly used as a flavoring agent in various food and beverage products. Cocoa paste, also known as cocoa liquor, is a smooth liquid made from grinding roasted cocoa beans, often used in chocolate manufacturing. Cocoa butter is the fat component extracted from cocoa beans, employed in the production of chocolate products and cosmetics. Lastly, cocoa beans are the raw materials used to produce all other cocoa products.</p></p>
