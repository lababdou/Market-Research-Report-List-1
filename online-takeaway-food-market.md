<p><p>Online takeaway food refers to the ordering and delivery of food through online platforms or mobile applications from various restaurants and food outlets. The convenience and ease of ordering food online, coupled with busy lifestyles and the increasing penetration of smartphones and the internet, have been driving the market's growth. These platforms act as intermediaries between customers and restaurants, providing a wide range of food options from multiple restaurants on a single platform. This allows customers to compare prices, read reviews, and make informed decisions, further enhancing the user experience.</p><p>Another emerging trend in the market is the rise of cloud kitchens or virtual restaurants. These are delivery-only establishments that operate without physical dining spaces and primarily serve online orders.
