<p><p>The outdoor ice-cream machine market includes two main types: multi-cylinder and single cylinder machines. Multi-cylinder machines have several freezing cylinders, which allow for the simultaneous production of different flavors or variations of ice cream. On the other hand, single cylinder machines have only one freezing cylinder, limiting the production to a single flavor or variation at a time. Ultimately, the choice between these two types depends on the desired production capacity and variety of ice cream flavors offered by the business.</p></p>
