<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The wire binding machine market consists of two popular types: 2:1 (21 holes) and 3:1 (34 holes). In the 2:1 type, the machine punches 21 holes per inch, allowing for larger wire loops to bind documents. On the other hand, the 3:1 type punches 34 smaller holes per inch, resulting in smaller wire loops. Both types offer effective binding solutions for different document sizes and customer preferences.</p></p>
