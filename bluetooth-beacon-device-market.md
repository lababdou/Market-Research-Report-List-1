<p><ul><li>IBeacon</li><li>Eddystone</li><li>AltBeacon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth Beacon Device market is categorized into three major types: iBeacon, Eddystone, and AltBeacon. iBeacon is a proprietary protocol developed by Apple, primarily used with iOS devices. Eddystone, on the other hand, is an open-source protocol developed by Google, supporting both Android and iOS platforms. AltBeacon is an open-source and cross-platform protocol developed by Radius Networks. These types of Bluetooth beacons are used for location-based services, proximity marketing, asset tracking, and indoor navigation, with each protocol offering its own set of features and compatibility.</p></p>
