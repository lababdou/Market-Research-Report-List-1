<p><p>Fluorometers are widely used devices for measuring and analyzing fluorescence. There are two main types of Fluorometers, including Filter Fluorometers and Spectrofluorometers. Filter Fluorometers utilize filters to select specific wavelengths of light, enabling the measurement of fluorescence emission at specific wavelengths. On the other hand, Spectrofluorometers use a monochromator to scan a range of wavelengths, providing detailed fluorescence spectra for analysis. Both types of fluorometers have their specific applications and are important tools in fields such as biology, chemistry, environmental science, and materials science.</p></p>
