<p><p>The surgical medical mask market can be divided into two types: disposable masks and reusable masks. Disposable masks are designed for single-use and are made of a non-woven fabric that filters out potentially harmful particles. These masks are convenient and hygienic, as they can be easily discarded after use. Reusable masks, on the other hand, are made of durable materials that can be washed and reused multiple times. They provide a more sustainable option and often come with replaceable filters for improved filtration efficiency. Both types of masks play a crucial role in preventing the spread of airborne infections.</p></p>
