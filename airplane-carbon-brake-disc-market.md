<p><strong>The Airplane Carbon Brake Disc Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD</li><li>Short Fiber Impregnated Carbonization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airplane Carbon Brake Disc market is segmented into two types, namely Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Short Fiber Impregnated Carbonization. CVD involves depositing a layer of carbon on the disc through a chemical process, resulting in a strong and durable brake disc. On the other hand, Short Fiber Impregnated Carbonization involves impregnating short carbon fibers into the disc material, enhancing its overall strength and performance. Both these technologies play a crucial role in the manufacturing of high-quality and reliable carbon brake discs for airplanes.</p></p>
