<p><p>Semiconductor wafer transfer robots are designed for efficiently handling delicate wafers during the production process. There are two main types of market for these robots: atmospheric manipulators and vacuum manipulators. Atmospheric manipulators are used in environments with normal air pressure, while vacuum manipulators are used in low-pressure environments to prevent contamination and ensure precise handling of wafers. Both types of robots play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by facilitating the transfer of wafers between processing steps with utmost precision and accuracy.</p></p>
