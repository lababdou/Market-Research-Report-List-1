<p><p>The diamond grit tools market can be divided into various types based on their bonding material. Metal-bonded tools use a mixture of diamond particles with metal powders, providing durability and high cutting capacity. Plated tools have diamond particles electroplated onto a metal substrate, offering precision and long life. Ceramic-bonded tools feature diamond particles bonded with a ceramic material, delivering excellent heat resistance and wear resistance. Other types may include resin-bonded tools or hybrid-bonded tools, offering unique properties suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
