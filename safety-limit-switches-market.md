<p><p>Safety limit switches are an essential component in industrial settings to ensure the safety of workers and equipment. Mechanical switches are activated by physical contact, while non-contact switches utilize technologies like infrared sensors. In the Type II market, these switches are designed for specific applications that require extra protection, such as explosive environments or extreme temperatures. They provide added safety measures, minimizing the risk of accidents and protecting valuable machinery.</p></p>
