<p><p>An absorbable ligating clip is a surgical device used for clamping or ligating blood vessels or other tubular structures during surgical procedures. These clips are made from biocompatible materials that break down over time and are absorbed by the body, eliminating the need for clip removal surgery. They are commonly used in various surgical specialties such as general surgery, gynecology, urology, and orthopedics.</p><p>The absorbable ligating clip market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period. The market growth is primarily driven by the rising number of surgical procedures worldwide and the advantages offered by absorbable ligating clips over conventional metallic clips.</p><p>Absorbable ligating clips provide a safer and more convenient option for surgeons as they eliminate the risk of clip migration or damage to surrounding tissues during removal. They also reduce the chances of post-operative complications, such as granuloma formation, infection, or scarring. These benefits have increased the demand for absorbable ligating clips among surgeons, leading to market growth.</p><p>In addition, technological advancements and product innovations have further fueled the growth of the absorbable ligating clip market.
