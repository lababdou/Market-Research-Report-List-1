<p><p>The engine timing belt market is categorized into two types: rubber belt and PU (polyurethane) belt. Rubber belts are commonly used in most vehicles, as they offer good heat resistance and durability. They are cost-effective and provide reliable performance. On the other hand, PU belts are relatively newer in the market and offer advantages like higher tensile strength and reduced noise. They are known for improved power transmission efficiency and are often used in high-performance or luxury vehicles. Both rubber and PU belts play a crucial role in synchronizing the engine's internal components for efficient operation.</p></p>
