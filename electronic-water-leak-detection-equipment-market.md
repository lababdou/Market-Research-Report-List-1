<p><strong>The Electronic Water Leak Detection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positioning Type</li><li>Non-positioning Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Water Leak Detection Equipment market is categorized into two types: Positioning Type and Non-positioning Type. Positioning Type equipment utilizes sensors or probes installed at specific locations to detect water leaks and their exact position within a facility. This type of equipment offers precise and targeted leak detection, making it suitable for large-scale facilities. Non-positioning Type equipment, on the other hand, does not require specific installation and can detect leaks over a larger area. This type is generally more cost-effective and easier to maintain, making it ideal for small to medium-sized facilities.</p></p>
