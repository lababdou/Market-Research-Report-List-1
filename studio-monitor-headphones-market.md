<p><p>Studio monitor headphones are categorized into different types based on their design. The first type is based on how they are worn: in-ear and over-head. In-ear headphones fit directly into the ear canal, while over-head headphones cover the entire ear. The second type is based on the design of the cup: closed-back, open, and semi-open. Closed-back headphones have a sealed cup that isolates sound, open headphones have an open cup allowing sound leakage, and semi-open headphones strike a balance between the two. These different types cater to different preferences and studio requirements.</p></p>
