<p><p>The veneer knives market comprises various types of knives used in the woodworking industry. Slicing knives are designed specifically for cutting thin slices of wood veneer, ensuring precision and smooth edges. Clipper knives are used for trimming and shaping the veneer edges, providing accuracy during the process. Peeling knives are utilized to remove veneer from logs or blocks, allowing for easy extraction without damaging the wood. Other types of veneer knives include core knives, perforated knives, and guillotine knives, offering diverse functionalities to meet different woodworking needs.</p></p>
