<p><p>Next generation optical biometry devices market is segmented into Swept Source Optical Coherent Based Optical Biometers, Partial Coherence Interferometry (PCI) Based Optical Biometers, and Optical Low Coherence Reflectometry (OLCR) Based Optical Biometers. Swept source optical biometers utilize a swept laser as the light source, offering high speed and accuracy in measuring axial length. PCI based biometers use a low coherence interferometry technique for precise measurements. OLCR based biometers offer high resolution and imaging capabilities, enabling detailed assessment of ocular structures.</p></p>
