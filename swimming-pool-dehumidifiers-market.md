<p><p>Swimming pool dehumidifiers are categorized into two main types: portable dehumidifiers and whole-home dehumidifiers. Portable dehumidifiers are compact and can be easily moved around as needed. They are suitable for small to medium-sized pools or specific areas within larger pools. On the other hand, whole-home dehumidifiers are designed to regulate the humidity levels of an entire pool area or facility. These types of dehumidifiers are generally larger and more powerful, capable of maintaining a consistent humidity level throughout the entire pool area.</p></p>
