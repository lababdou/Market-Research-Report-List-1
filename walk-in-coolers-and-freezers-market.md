<p><p>Walk-in coolers and freezers are available in three main market types: </p><p>1. Self-contained units have the refrigeration system built into the structure, making them easy to install and relocate.</p><p>2. Remote condensing units have the refrigeration system located outside of the structure, reducing noise and heat inside the cooler or freezer.</p><p>3. Multiplex condensing units allow for multiple units to be connected to one condensing system, making them a cost-effective option for larger installations. Each market type offers different benefits and options for customers based on their needs.</p></p>
