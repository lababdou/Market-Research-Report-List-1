<p><p>The CMP filter market is categorized into two main types: cartridge filter and capsule filter. Cartridge filters consist of a cylindrical housing filled with a filter medium, allowing for high flow rates and efficient filtration. On the other hand, capsule filters have a compact design with a small disposable unit that houses the filter medium. They are suitable for small-scale applications and offer ease of installation. Both types efficiently remove particulates from chemical mechanical planarization (CMP) processes, ensuring improved product quality and process efficiency in industries such as electronics and semiconductors.</p></p>
