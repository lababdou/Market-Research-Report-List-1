<p><p>The concrete cutting machine market comprises three main types: handheld, cut-off, and walk-behind-push. Handheld machines are portable and designed for smaller cutting tasks, offering flexibility and maneuverability. Cut-off machines are commonly used for creating straight cuts in concrete, asphalt, and other materials. They are typically equipped with a diamond blade and provide accurate and precise cuts. Walk-behind-push machines are larger and more powerful, suitable for heavy-duty cutting of concrete surfaces such as floors and roads. They offer stability and ease of use while maintaining efficiency.</p></p>
