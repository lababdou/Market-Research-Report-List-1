<p><p>Wall-mounted extraction arms are devices used to capture and remove airborne contaminants, such as dust, fumes, and gases, from a work environment. These arms are typically mounted on walls, allowing for flexible positioning and easy retraction when not in use. The extracted contaminants are then filtered and removed from the air, improving the overall air quality and ensuring a healthy and safe working environment.</p><p>The market for wall-mounted extraction arms is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing focus on workplace safety and regulations regarding occupational health and safety are driving the demand for efficient and effective extraction systems. Industries such as manufacturing, welding, pharmaceuticals, and automotive are particularly adopting these systems to maintain a clean and healthy work environment.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative extraction arm designs, such as longer reach arms and arms with improved flexibility. These advancements are further fueling the market growth by providing enhanced performance and user-friendliness.</p><p>Another trend observed in the wall-mounted extraction arms market is the growing adoption of energy-efficient systems.
