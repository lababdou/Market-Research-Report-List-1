<p><p>A medical ankle walker, also known as an air cast or walking boot, is a medical device used to support and immobilize the ankle and foot during the healing process of injuries or surgeries. It is a brace that extends from the foot to below the knee, providing protection, stability, and mobility to the user while allowing them to walk. Ankle walkers are commonly used for conditions such as fractures, sprains, strains, and post-surgical recovery.</p><p>The global medical ankle walker market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period. <p><p>The medical ankle walker market offers two main types: low height and high height walkers.
