<p><p>The photovoltaic market is categorized into three types: Thin Film, Mono Si, and Multi Si. Thin Film technology involves the deposition of a light-absorbing material onto a substrate to convert sunlight into electricity. Mono Si (monocrystalline silicon) market employs single-crystal silicon wafers that offer higher efficiency levels. Multi Si (multicrystalline silicon) market uses silicon with multiple crystal structures, providing a cost-effective alternative. These three market types differ in terms of production methods, efficiency, and cost, allowing consumers to choose based on their specific requirements and budget.</p></p>
