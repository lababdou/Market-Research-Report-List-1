<p><ul><li>Pump Coronary Artery Bypass</li><li>Minimally Invasive Direct Coronary Artery Bypass</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Beating Heart Surgery System market includes various types, such as Pump Coronary Artery Bypass (CAB), Minimally Invasive Direct Coronary Artery Bypass (MIDCAB), and Others. Pump CAB utilizes a heart-lung machine to temporarily take over heart functions during surgery, allowing surgeons to operate on a motionless heart. MIDCAB, on the other hand, involves smaller incisions and the use of robotic assistance, enabling surgeons to bypass blocked coronary arteries without stopping the heart. The "Others" category includes alternative techniques or emerging technologies used in beating heart surgeries.</p></p>
