<p><p>A lightning arrester, also known as a surge arrester or surge protection device (SPD), is a device used to protect electrical equipment from the damaging effects of lightning strikes and power surges. Lightning arresters are typically installed on electrical systems such as power transmission lines, electrical substations, and buildings, where they divert the excessive electrical energy from lightning strikes or power surges to the ground, preventing it from reaching and damaging the connected equipment.</p><p>The lightning arrester market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The growing need to protect electrical infrastructure from lightning-related damages is a major factor driving the market growth. With the increasing adoption of electrical equipment and systems in various industries, the demand for lightning arresters is also increasing to ensure the smooth functioning and longevity of such systems.</p><p>Moreover, the rapid urbanization and industrialization, particularly in developing economies, are contributing to the market growth. The expansion of power transmission and distribution networks in these regions is creating a considerable demand for lightning arresters.</p><p>Technological advancements in lightning arrester designs and materials are also playing a crucial role in the market growth. Manufacturers are focusing on developing more efficient and durable lightning arresters to cater to the evolving needs of various industries. Factors such as the increasing investment in power infrastructure and the growing awareness regarding the importance of surge protection are likely to drive the market further.
