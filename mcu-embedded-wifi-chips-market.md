<p><p>The MCU-embedded WiFi Chips market can be classified into two types: Single Band and Dual Band. Single Band WiFi chips operate on a single frequency band, typically 2.4 GHz, providing lower data rates with limited interference. On the other hand, Dual Band WiFi chips operate on both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequency bands, enabling higher data rates and reduced interference in congested areas. Dual Band chips offer better performance and flexibility in connecting to different networks, while Single Band chips are more cost-effective for simpler applications.</p></p>
