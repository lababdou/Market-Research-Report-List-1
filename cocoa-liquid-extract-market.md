<p><p>The cocoa liquid extract market consists of various types, including white cocoa liquid extract, milk cocoa liquid extract, and dark cocoa liquid extract. White cocoa liquid extract is produced by using roasted cocoa beans with their husks removed, resulting in a smooth and sweet flavor. Milk cocoa liquid extract incorporates dairy ingredients, providing a creamier taste ideal for milk-based beverages and desserts. Dark cocoa liquid extract is made from cocoa beans with a higher percentage of cocoa solids, giving it a rich and intense flavor suitable for indulgent products like dark chocolate or espresso-based drinks.</p></p>
