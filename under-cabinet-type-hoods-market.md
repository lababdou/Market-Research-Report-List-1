<p><p>Under Cabinet Type Hoods are ventilation systems used in kitchens to remove smoke, odors, and grease generated during cooking. Axial Flow Hood is a market type that uses propeller-like blades to draw air through a narrow space, ideal for light cooking. On the other hand, Centrifugal Hood uses a fan to create centrifugal force, pushing air to the outer edges before expelling it, suitable for heavy cooking. Both hoods are designed to fit underneath a cabinet, maximizing space efficiency and providing a cleaner cooking environment.</p></p>
