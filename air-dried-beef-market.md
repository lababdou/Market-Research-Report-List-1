<p><p>Air-dried beef is a popular type of processed meat that comes in two main market types: cooked processing and uncooked processing. Cooked processing involves partially cooking the beef before it is air-dried, which helps to remove possible pathogens and extend its shelf life. Uncooked processing, on the other hand, involves air-drying raw beef without any cooking process. Both market types offer different tastes and textures, with cooked processing being softer and milder, while uncooked processing results in a denser and more intense flavor.</p></p>
