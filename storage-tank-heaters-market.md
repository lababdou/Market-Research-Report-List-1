<p><ul><li>Direct Heat Tank Heaters</li><li>Direct Immersion Tank Heaters</li><li>Indirect Immersion Tank Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Storage tank heaters are used to heat fluids in large industrial storage tanks. There are three main types of tank heaters. Direct heat tank heaters use a flame or electric heating element inserted directly into the tank. Direct immersion tank heaters involve placing the heating element into the fluid itself, while indirect immersion tank heaters use a separate heat transfer medium, such as steam or hot water, to heat the fluid without direct contact. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
