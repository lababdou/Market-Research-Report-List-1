<p><p>Fluoropolymer Heat Exchangers are specialized equipment used for transferring heat between fluids in various applications. There are several types available, including Shell and Tube Heat Exchangers, Reactor Coil Heat Exchangers, and Immersion Coil Heat Exchangers. Shell and Tube Heat Exchangers consist of multiple tubes inside a shell, allowing for efficient heat transfer. Reactor Coil Heat Exchangers are coiled tubes within a reactor vessel, specifically designed for heating or cooling chemical reactions. Immersion Coil Heat Exchangers are immersed directly into a liquid, facilitating heat exchange with the surrounding medium.</p></p>
