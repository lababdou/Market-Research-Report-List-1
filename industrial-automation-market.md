<p><p>Industrial Automation market types are various systems used to automate industrial processes. Computer Numerical Control (CNC) Routers enable precise machine control for tasks like cutting and shaping materials. Machine Vision Systems use cameras and software for quality inspection and process control. Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) monitor and manage production, while Plant Asset Management tracks and optimizes equipment performance. Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) manages product development from inception to disposal. Programmable Logic Control Systems (PLC) automate machinery operations. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems gather real-time data for monitoring and control. Other systems include robotics, motion control, and human-machine interface.</p></p>
