<p><ul><li>Paper-coated Product</li><li>Thermoforming Product</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biodegradable cup market comprises two main types: paper-coated products and thermoforming products. Paper-coated products are cups made from a layer of paper that is coated with a biodegradable material, providing rigidity and preventing liquid leakage. Thermoforming products, on the other hand, are cups produced by heating and molding a biodegradable material into the desired shape. Both types aim to provide eco-friendly alternatives to traditional plastic cups, as they break down naturally over time, minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
