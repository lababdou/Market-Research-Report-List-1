<p><p>Harbour dredging is the process of removing sediment and debris from a harbor bed to ensure safe navigation of ships and boats. There are several types of market segments in the harbour dredging industry. Capital dredging involves deepening or widening a harbor for new infrastructure or port development. Coastal protection dredging aims to counteract erosion and maintain or enhance the coastline's natural defenses. Maintenance dredging is performed regularly to remove accumulated sediment and maintain shipping channels. Finally, rivers and lakes dredging helps maintain waterways for commercial and recreational use by removing sediment and improving access.</p></p>
