<p><p>The handheld engraving machine market consists of two main types: mechanical engraving and laser engraving. Mechanical engraving machines use a physical tool, such as a rotating cutter or diamond-tipped stylus, to carve designs or lettering into a surface. These machines are commonly used for engraving on harder materials like metal or wood. On the other hand, laser engraving machines utilize a high-powered laser beam to etch precise and intricate designs onto various materials, including metal, glass, plastic, and even organic substances like leather or wood. Laser engraving offers greater flexibility and accuracy but can be more expensive.</p></p>
