<p><p>Interventional pulmonology treatment market includes various types of equipment used to diagnose and treat respiratory diseases. Bronchoscopes are instruments used for visual examination and biopsy of the airways. Thoracoscopes aid in examining the pleural space and performing procedures like biopsy and drainage. Respiratory endotherapy devices help in removing foreign bodies and treating benign or malignant airway strictures. Airway stents are used to keep the airways open in cases of narrowing or blockage. Pleural catheters help drain fluid or air from the pleural cavity. Endobronchial valves assist in treating lung conditions by redirecting airflow.</p></p>
