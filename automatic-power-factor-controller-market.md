<p><strong>The Automatic Power Factor Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Power Factor Controller</li><li>Passive Power Factor Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for Automatic Power Factor Controllers can be categorized into two types: Active Power Factor Controller and Passive Power Factor Controller. </p><p>Active Power Factor Controllers are electronic devices that constantly monitor the power factor of the electrical system and automatically adjust it to maintain a near-unity power factor. They achieve this by injecting or absorbing reactive power as needed. </p><p>On the other hand, Passive Power Factor Controllers are simpler devices that use passive components like capacitors or inductors to correct the power factor. They are less expensive but offer limited control over reactive power correction.</p></p>
