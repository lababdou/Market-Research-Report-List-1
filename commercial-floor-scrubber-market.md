<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The commercial floor scrubber market consists of three main types: walk-behind floor scrubbers, ride-on floor scrubbers, and stand-on floor scrubbers. Walk-behind floor scrubbers are operated by a person walking behind the machine, making them suitable for smaller areas. Ride-on floor scrubbers allow the operator to sit and drive the machine, which is ideal for larger spaces. Stand-on floor scrubbers offer a balance between the two, where the operator stands on the machine for improved visibility and maneuverability. Each type caters to different cleaning needs and area sizes in commercial settings.</p></p>
