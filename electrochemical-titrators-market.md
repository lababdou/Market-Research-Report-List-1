<p><strong>The Electrochemical Titrators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Titrator</li><li>Volumetric Titrator</li><li>Coulometric Titrator</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrochemical titrators are instruments used in the laboratory to determine the concentration of a chemical in a solution. There are several types of electrochemical titrators available in the market. </p><p>Potentiometric titrators measure the potential difference between two electrodes and use it to calculate concentration. Volumetric titrators measure the volume of a reactant required to complete a reaction and determine concentration. Coulometric titrators measure the quantity of electricity required to complete a reaction and calculate concentration. Other types of electrochemical titrators include amperometric titrators, Karl Fischer titrators, and pH stat titrators. Each type is suited for specific applications and offers different benefits to the user.</p></p>
