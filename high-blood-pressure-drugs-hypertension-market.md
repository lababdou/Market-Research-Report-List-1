<p><p>High blood pressure drugs are used to treat hypertension. There are three main types of drugs in the market - ACE inhibitors (ACEI), calcium channel blockers (CCB), and angiotensin receptor blockers (ARB). ACE inhibitors work by blocking the enzyme responsible for narrowing blood vessels, thus reducing blood pressure. Calcium channel blockers relax and widen blood vessels, allowing improved blood flow. Angiotensin receptor blockers also relax blood vessels by blocking the effects of a hormone that narrows them. These drugs help manage high blood pressure and reduce the risk of associated complications.</p></p>
