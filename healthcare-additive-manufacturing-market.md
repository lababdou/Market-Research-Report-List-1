<p><p>Healthcare additive manufacturing, also known as 3D printing in healthcare, refers to the process of creating three-dimensional objects that are specifically designed for medical applications. Additionally, the ability of additive manufacturing to reduce production time and cost, minimize the risk of error, and improve patient outcomes further contributes to its market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its growth. For instance, the use of 3D printing in personalized drug delivery systems and pharmaceutical research has gained traction.
