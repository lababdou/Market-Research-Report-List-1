<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metagenomic Sequencing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shotgun Metagenomic Sequencing</li><li>16S rRNA Sequencing</li><li>Metatranscriptomics</li><li>Whole-Genome Sequencing & De Novo Assembly</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for metagenomic sequencing services can be classified into four types. Shotgun metagenomic sequencing involves randomly sequencing fragments of DNA from a complex mixture, providing information about the genetic composition and function of the sample. 16S rRNA sequencing targets a specific gene region to analyze the microbial communities present. Metatranscriptomics focuses on the gene expression of organisms in a sample, providing insights into their functional activities. Whole-genome sequencing covers the entire genome of an organism, enabling comprehensive genetic analysis. De novo assembly involves reconstructing the genome sequence from short sequencing reads without a reference genome.</p></p>
