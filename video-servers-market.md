<p><p>The video server market encompasses technologies like M-JPEG, MPEG-4, and H.264. M-JPEG technology uses motion JPEG compression to efficiently send and receive video data over networks, serving applications requiring high image quality. MPEG-4 technology provides video compression, allowing for reduced bandwidth usage while maintaining good video quality. Lastly, H.264 technology offers advanced video compression capabilities, supporting high-quality video streaming over limited bandwidth networks. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, contributing to the diverse video server market.</p></p>
