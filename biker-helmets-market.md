<p><p>The biker helmets market includes several types of helmets designed for different purposes. Full face helmets provide the most coverage, protecting the entire head and face. Half face helmets cover the top of the head and do not have a chin bar. Modular helmets offer the versatility of a full face helmet with the option to flip up the front to expose the face. Off-road helmets are designed specifically for off-road riding, providing additional protection for the chin and visor. Open face helmets cover the top and sides of the head but leave the face exposed. Lastly, there are other types of specialized helmets available for specific biking activities.</p></p>
