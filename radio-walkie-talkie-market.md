<p><p>The Radio Walkie-Talkie market consists of two main types: Simplex and Duplex. Simplex Radio Walkie-Talkies allow communication in only one direction at a time, similar to a conversation on a phone. They are commonly used for short-range communication, such as in restaurants or security teams. On the other hand, Duplex Radio Walkie-Talkies allow two-way communication simultaneously, much like a traditional phone call. They have longer range capabilities and are often used in industries like construction and emergency services that require constant communication among team members.</p></p>
