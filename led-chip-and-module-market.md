<p><p>LED chips and modules refer to the components used in LED lighting systems. The market is segmented into three types: lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip. Lateral chip technology places the electrical contacts on the same surface as the active area, allowing for better heat dissipation. Vertical chip technology arranges the contacts on opposite surfaces of the chip, enabling higher current handling capacity. Flip chip technology flips the LED chip and attaches it face down, offering improved luminous efficiency and reliability. These different market types cater to various lighting applications and customer needs.</p></p>
