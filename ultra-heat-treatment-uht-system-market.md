<p><p>Ultra Heat Treatment (UHT) system market has two main types: Direct Heating UHT System and Indirect Heating UHT System. Direct Heating UHT System involves the direct injection of steam into the product, resulting in rapid heating and sterilization. On the other hand, Indirect Heating UHT System uses a heat exchanger to heat the product indirectly, providing more control over the heating process. Both systems are widely used in the food and beverage industry for extending the shelf life of products.</p></p>
