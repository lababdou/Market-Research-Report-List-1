<p><p>The medical dermatomes market includes different types of devices used to harvest skin grafts during surgical procedures. Electric dermatomes are powered by electricity and use a rotating blade to remove thin layers of skin. Pneumatic dermatomes, on the other hand, are powered by compressed air and operate using a reciprocating blade. Other types of dermatomes may include those powered by battery or gas. These different types of dermatomes offer surgeons various options to choose from based on their preferences and the specific needs of the procedure.</p></p>
