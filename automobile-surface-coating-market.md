<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Water-borne</li><li>Powder Coatin</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile surface coating market types include solvent-borne, water-borne, powder coating, and others. Solvent-borne coatings contain volatile organic compounds that evaporate into the air during application, posing environmental concerns. Water-borne coatings are environmentally friendly as they use water as a solvent. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and cured under heat to form a protective layer. Other coatings may include UV-cured coatings or ceramic coatings with unique properties for specific applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
