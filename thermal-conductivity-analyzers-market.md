Integration analyzers are compact and designed to be easily integrated into existing systems or processes for continuous monitoring. Portable analyzers, on the other hand, are smaller, lightweight, and easily transportable, making them suitable for field applications or on-site measurements. Each type caters to different needs and environments within the thermal conductivity analysis market.</p></p>
