<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Auto Drive System market consists of three main types: Perception System, Decision-Making System, and Control Execution System. The Perception System utilizes various sensors such as cameras and radars to detect and interpret the surrounding environment. The Decision-Making System then analyzes this data and determines the appropriate actions for the vehicle. Finally, the Control Execution System implements these decisions by controlling the vehicle's acceleration, braking, and steering. These three systems work together to enable autonomous driving, ensuring a safe and efficient driving experience.</p></p>

L2 systems offer enhanced capabilities by combining multiple features such as automated steering, braking, and acceleration. L3 systems allow partial automation, where the driver's attention is needed but can hand over control in specific conditions. L4 systems bring high automation, allowing the vehicle to perform most driving tasks independently, with minimal interaction from the driver.</p></p>
