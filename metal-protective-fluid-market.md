<p><p>Metal protective fluids are classified into three main types based on their composition: biological base, mineral fluid, and synthetic liquid. Biological base fluids are derived from natural sources such as plants or microbes and provide eco-friendly protection. Mineral fluids are derived from petroleum or mineral oil and offer excellent heat resistance. Synthetic liquids are artificially produced using chemical processes to meet specific performance requirements. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications in the metal protection industry.</p></p>
