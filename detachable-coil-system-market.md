<p><strong>The Detachable Coil System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrolytic Detachable Coil System</li><li>Mechanical Detachable Coil System</li><li>Hydraulic Detachable Coil System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Detachable coil systems are utilized in endovascular procedures to treat cerebral aneurysms. Electrolytic detachable coil systems involve electrolysis to detach the coil from its delivery wire. Mechanical detachable coil systems comprise a mechanical mechanism to separate the coil from the delivery wire. Hydraulic detachable coil systems use a hydraulic system to detach the coil. These three types of detachable coil systems provide different methods to securely place and detach the coil during aneurysm treatment, offering physicians options to choose the most suitable system based on individual patient needs.</p></p>
