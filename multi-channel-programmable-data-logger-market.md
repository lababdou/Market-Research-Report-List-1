<p><p>The multi-channel programmable data logger market is divided into two types: wireless and wired. The wireless market refers to data loggers that can collect and transmit data without the need for physical connections. They use technologies like Bluetooth or Wi-Fi to communicate with other devices. On the other hand, the wired market consists of data loggers that require physical connections, usually through cables or wires, to both collect and transfer data. Both types offer different advantages, with wireless loggers providing more flexibility and convenience, while wired loggers offer a more stable and reliable connection.</p></p>
