There are three types of mixer seals: single seal, double seal, and split seal. </p><p>- Single seals are the most basic type and consist of one sealing element between the rotating and stationary parts.</p><p>- Double seals have two independent sealing elements, offering an extra level of protection.</p><p>- Split seals are designed with a split casing, allowing for easy installation and maintenance while maintaining effective sealing.
