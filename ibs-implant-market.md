<p><strong>The IBS Implant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screwed IBS Implant</li><li>Non-screwed IBS Implant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IBS Implant market consists of two types: Screwed IBS Implants and Non-screwed IBS Implants. Screwed IBS Implants are designed to be inserted into the bone using screws for stability. They offer a secure fit and are commonly used in procedures where maximum support is required. On the other hand, Non-screwed IBS Implants do not require screws for fixation. They utilize other mechanisms like friction or press-fit to achieve stability. Non-screwed implants are often used in situations where preserving the bone structure is crucial or when screws cannot be used due to specific patient conditions.</p></p>
