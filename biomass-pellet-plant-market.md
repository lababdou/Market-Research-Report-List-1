<p><p>The biomass pellet plant market includes various types, such as wood, sawdust, rice husk, straw, and others. Wood biomass pellets are derived from various wood sources, like lumber and timber industries. Sawdust pellets are made from sawdust waste generated by sawmills and woodworking factories. Rice husk pellets utilize agricultural waste produced during rice milling. Straw pellets are created from agricultural straw, typically leftover after harvesting crops like wheat, corn, or rice. Lastly, the "others" category consists of biomass pellets obtained from various sources, such as corn cobs, coconut shells, and grasses.</p></p>
