<p><p>The Automatic Tag Reader market comprises two major types, RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) and OCR (Optical Character Recognition). RFID tag readers use radio waves to identify and track objects, facilitating inventory management, access control, and supply chain optimization. On the other hand, OCR tag readers employ image processing technology to recognize and interpret characters or codes on tags, enabling efficient data extraction and automation in various industries. Both types play a significant role in improving operational efficiency and accuracy in different contexts such as logistics, retail, and transportation.</p></p>
