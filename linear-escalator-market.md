<p><p>In the linear escalator market, there are two main types based on their load capacity: light and heavy. Light linear escalators are designed for relatively low weight capacities and are commonly found in residential buildings, small commercial spaces, and low-foot traffic areas. On the other hand, heavy linear escalators are designed to accommodate higher load capacities and are typically installed in high-rise buildings, airports, shopping malls, and other areas with high foot traffic and heavy usage requirements.</p></p>
