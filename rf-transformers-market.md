<p><p>RF transformers are electromagnetic devices that transfer energy between two or more radio frequency circuits. They are classified based on the frequency range they operate in. Low-frequency RF transformers typically handle frequencies up to a few hundred kilohertz. Medium-frequency RF transformers work with frequencies up to a few megahertz, while high-frequency RF transformers operate in the range of several megahertz to several gigahertz. Each type is designed to optimize performance based on the specific frequency range it caters to, ensuring efficient energy transfer between RF circuits.</p></p>
