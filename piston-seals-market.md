<p><p>Piston seals are used to prevent leakage of fluid between the piston and cylinder wall in hydraulic and pneumatic systems. There are three main types of piston seals in the market. Double-acting piston seals are used in both directions, allowing fluid to pass in either direction. Single-acting piston seals are designed for use in one direction only. Custom-designed piston seals are tailored for specific applications based on unique requirements. These seals are vital in maintaining the efficiency and reliability of hydraulic and pneumatic systems.</p></p>
