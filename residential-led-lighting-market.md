<p><p>The residential LED lighting market consists of two main types: replacement lamps and luminaires. Replacement lamps refer to LED bulbs that can be used to replace traditional incandescent or compact fluorescent bulbs in existing fixtures. These bulbs are designed to fit into standard sockets and provide energy-efficient lighting. On the other hand, luminaires are complete lighting fixtures that come with integrated LED technology. They are typically installed as a whole unit and offer a wide range of designs and functionalities to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of residential spaces.</p></p>
