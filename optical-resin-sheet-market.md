<p><strong>The Optical Resin Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Material</li><li>Thermoplastic Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical resin sheets are classified into two main types based on the material they are made from: thermosetting and thermoplastic. Thermosetting material types of optical resin sheets are cured using heat and pressure, resulting in a rigid and stable structure. In contrast, thermoplastic material types can be heated and reshaped multiple times without losing their properties. Both types have unique qualities that make them suitable for various applications in the optical industry, offering a wide range of options for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
