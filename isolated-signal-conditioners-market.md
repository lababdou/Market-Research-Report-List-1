<p><p>Isolated signal conditioners are devices used to convert and isolate electrical signals in various industrial applications. The market for these conditioners can be categorized into three types.</p><p>Single channel: This type involves conditioners that process and isolate a single input signal at a time. They are ideal for applications where only one signal needs to be converted and protected.</p><p>Dual channel: These conditioners are designed to handle two input signals simultaneously. They are commonly used in applications where two independent signals require conversion and isolation.</p><p>Multi channel: This category includes conditioners capable of processing and isolating multiple input signals simultaneously. They are suitable for applications that involve multiple signals that need to be converted and protected separately.</p></p>
