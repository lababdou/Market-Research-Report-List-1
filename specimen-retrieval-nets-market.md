<p><p>Specimen retrieval nets are medical devices used during minimally invasive surgeries to retrieve tissue samples or organs from the body. There are two main types of these nets: detachable and non-detachable. Detachable specimen retrieval nets allow for easy removal of the specimen by disconnecting it from the rest of the apparatus, while non-detachable nets do not have this feature and require the entire device to be removed from the body. Both types offer benefits and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
