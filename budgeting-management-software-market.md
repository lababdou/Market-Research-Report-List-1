<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Budgeting management software can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. </p><p>Cloud-based software is hosted on external servers and accessed through the internet. It allows users to access and manage their budgets from anywhere with an internet connection, providing flexibility and real-time collaboration. This type of software often requires a subscription fee but eliminates the need for additional hardware or IT maintenance.</p><p>On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed and run on the user's own servers or computers. It offers more control and data security but may require investments in hardware and IT resources for installation, maintenance, and updates.</p></p>
