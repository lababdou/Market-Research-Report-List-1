<p><p>Vacuum blood tubes are widely used in medical laboratories and clinics for blood collection and analysis. There are various types of vacuum blood tubes available in the market. Serum separating tubes are designed to separate blood clot from serum. EDTA tubes have an anticoagulant called ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid which is used for various blood tests. Plasma separation tubes contain separator gels or particles to separate plasma from blood cells. Other types of vacuum blood tubes include heparin tubes, glucose tubes, and clot activator tubes, each used for specific tests and purposes.</p></p>
