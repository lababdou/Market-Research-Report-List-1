<p><p>Injection molding machine servo system is a type of motion control system used in injection molding machines. It utilizes a servo motor to control the motion of the machine's hydraulic system, which in turn controls the movement of the mold and the injection of molten material.</p><p>The servo system offers several advantages over traditional hydraulic systems, including higher energy efficiency, improved precision and accuracy, reduced noise levels, and faster response times. These benefits make it a preferred choice for manufacturers looking to increase productivity and reduce operational costs.</p><p>The injection molding machine servo system market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period. <p><p>Injection molding machine servo systems can be classified into two types: low power servo systems and high power servo systems. </p><p>Low power servo systems are designed for small to medium-sized injection molding machines. They are more energy-efficient and cost-effective compared to high power servo systems. These systems provide precise control over the machine's movements, resulting in higher product quality and productivity.</p><p>On the other hand, high power servo systems are suitable for large-sized injection molding machines. They have a higher power output and are capable of handling heavy loads. They offer enhanced hydraulic performance, allowing for faster and more efficient injection molding processes.</p><p>Overall, both low power and high power servo systems play crucial roles in the injection molding industry, catering to different machine sizes and application requirements.</p></p>
