These crystals have specific properties that allow them to transmit infrared radiation while remaining resistant to temperature changes and environmental factors, making them suitable for various applications, including thermal imaging and remote sensing.</p><p>Polymer Material Market: In this market type, infrared windows are made using polymer materials such as polyethylene or polycarbonate. These materials are lightweight, durable, and cost-effective, making them ideal for mass production and widespread use. While they may have lower optical clarity compared to crystal materials, polymer infrared windows still provide sufficient infrared transmission for applications in building inspections, electrical maintenance, and other non-critical thermal imaging tasks.</p></p>
