<p><p>The suction blower market offers different types of blowers, such as spiral fans and centrifugal fans. Spiral fans are designed with curved blades that rotate inside a spiral-shaped housing, creating a high static pressure and low flow rate. They are typically used in applications where air circulation is required within limited spaces. On the other hand, centrifugal fans feature straight blades that spin on a central hub, generating a medium static pressure and high flow rate. They are commonly utilized in situations where a larger volume of air movement is necessary.</p></p>
