<p><p>Boot sole washers are available in two main types: walk-through type and upright type. The walk-through type is designed for users to step onto a platform and walk through a washing mechanism that cleans the soles of their boots. It is commonly used in industries or facilities where hygiene is essential. The upright type, on the other hand, requires the user to manually hold and move the boots against the washing mechanism. This type is often used in smaller-scale applications or households where a walk-through version may not be necessary.</p></p>
