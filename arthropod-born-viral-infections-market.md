Some well-known arthropod-borne viral infections include dengue fever, Zika virus, West Nile virus, and Chikungunya.</p><p>The market for arthropod-borne viral infections is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The development of rapid diagnostic tests, improved laboratory techniques, and increased funding for research and development activities have enabled healthcare professionals to diagnose and manage these infections more effectively.</p><p>Moreover, the market growth is further attributed to the increasing focus on preventive measures such as vector control programs and vaccination campaigns.
