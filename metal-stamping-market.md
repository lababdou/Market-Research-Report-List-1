<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metal Stamping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blanking Process</li><li>Embossing Process</li><li>Bending Process</li><li>Coining Process</li><li>Flanging Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal stamping market comprises different types of processes to shape metal sheets into desired forms. The blanking process involves cutting flat shapes from metal sheets. The embossing process creates raised or depressed designs on metal surfaces. Bending process involves creating angular shapes in metal sheets. Coining process produces a permanent design on metal surfaces. Lastly, the flanging process creates edges or rims on metal sheets. These processes are widely used in various industries like automotive, aerospace, electronics, and construction to manufacture a wide range of metal products.</p></p>
