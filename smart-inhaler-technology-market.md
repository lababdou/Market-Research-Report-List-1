<p><p>Smart inhaler technology is an advanced solution designed to monitor and improve medication adherence among individuals suffering from respiratory diseases such as asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). Additionally, the rising adoption of digital healthcare solutions, advancements in mobile health technologies, and increasing healthcare expenditure further contribute to market growth.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends that are shaping its future. One such trend is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms into smart inhalers. These technologies enable the devices to learn and adapt to patients' behavior, monitor patterns, and provide more accurate insights for personalized treatment plans. Furthermore, the advent of connected healthcare ecosystems and the Internet of Things (IoT) has enabled real-time patient monitoring and has facilitated remote consultation and telemedicine services.</p><p>As per the analysis, the smart inhaler technology market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.1% during the forecast period. Factors such as the increasing awareness about the benefits of smart inhalers, improved healthcare infrastructure in developing economies, and the growing need for remote patient monitoring are expected to drive market growth. However, challenges related to data security and privacy, high costs of smart inhalers, and lack of awareness among patients in certain regions may hinder market growth to some extent.</p></p>
