<p><p>The dried apricot snack market can be categorized into three main types - sun dried, freeze dried, and other types. Sun dried apricots are created by naturally drying them under the sun, making them slightly chewy with a strong fruity flavor. Freeze dried apricots are made by rapidly freezing and then slowly removing moisture, resulting in a light and crispy texture while maintaining their natural taste.
