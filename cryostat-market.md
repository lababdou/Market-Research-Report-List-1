<p><p>The Cryostat market consists of various types, including Closed-Cycle Cryostats, Continuous-Flow Cryostats, Bath Cryostats, and Multistage Cryostats. Closed-Cycle Cryostats utilize refrigeration systems to cool samples without the need for external cooling. Continuous-Flow Cryostats use a continuous flow of cryogenic fluids to maintain low temperatures. Bath Cryostats immerse samples in a bath of cryogenic fluid to achieve desired temperatures. Multistage Cryostats employ multiple cooling stages to achieve ultra-low temperatures. These different types cater to various applications and research requirements, providing options for scientists and researchers in different fields.</p></p>
