They can perform various tasks, such as feeding, printing, drying, and stacking, automatically. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic machines still require some level of human involvement in operating and monitoring the printing process. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements and capabilities of the printing job.</p></p>
