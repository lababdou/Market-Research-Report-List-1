<p><p>Nano fibers market can be categorized into three types based on production methods: molecular technique, spinning method, and biological method. Molecular technique involves the direct synthesis of nanofibers from molecules. Spinning method utilizes mechanical or electrospinning techniques to form nanofibers from a polymer solution. Biological method involves the use of biological organisms to create nanofibers. Each of these methods offers unique advantages and applications in various industries including healthcare, electronics, and textiles.</p></p>
