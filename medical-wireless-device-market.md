<p><p>The medical wireless device market can be categorized into two main types: wearable devices and implantable devices. On the other hand, implantable devices are medical devices that are surgically implanted inside the body, such as pacemakers or insulin pumps, and are designed to continuously monitor and deliver specific treatments or therapies to patients. Both types of devices enable real-time monitoring and collection of patient data to support personalized healthcare.</p></p>
