<p><p>The Ultrasonic Aspirator market can be categorized into two main types: Standalone Ultrasonic Aspirator and Integrated Ultrasonic Aspirator. Standalone Ultrasonic Aspirators are independent devices used for dissecting and removing tissue during surgical procedures. They are portable and can be easily transported between operating rooms. On the other hand, Integrated Ultrasonic Aspirators are built into surgical systems and are primarily used in operating rooms where they are permanently installed. Both types of aspirators serve the same purpose of providing surgeons with precise and efficient tissue dissection capabilities.</p></p>
