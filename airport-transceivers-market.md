<p><ul><li>UHF Airport Transceivers</li><li>VHF Airport Transceivers</li><li>HF Airport Transceivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport transceivers are communication devices used in the aviation industry. UHF (Ultra High Frequency) airport transceivers operate on frequencies between 225 MHz and 400 MHz. They are commonly used for air traffic control and ground-to-air communication. VHF (Very High Frequency) airport transceivers operate on frequencies between 118 MHz and 136 MHz. They are used for air-to-air communication and serve as aircraft radios. HF (High Frequency) airport transceivers operate on frequencies between 2 MHz and 30 MHz. They are used for long-range communication and are particularly useful for remote areas or during emergencies.</p></p>
