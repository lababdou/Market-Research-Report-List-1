<p><p>The edge sensor market includes two main types: laser edge sensors and ultrasonic edge sensors. Laser edge sensors use laser beams to detect the edge or boundary of an object, providing precise measurements and high accuracy. Ultrasonic edge sensors, on the other hand, use ultrasonic waves to detect objects' edges by measuring the time it takes for the waves to bounce back. These sensors are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing and robotics to ensure accurate positioning and detection of edges.</p></p>
