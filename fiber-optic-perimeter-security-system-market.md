<p><p>A fiber optic perimeter security system is a technology that uses fiber optic cables to create a secure perimeter around a specific area, such as a building or a campus. It uses the principles of light transmission through fiber optics to detect any intrusion attempts or breaches in the perimeter.</p><p>The system consists of fiber optic sensors that are buried underground or installed along fences, walls, or other boundaries. These sensors can detect any disturbances or changes in the light signal transmitted through the fiber optic cables, indicating the presence of an intruder. The system can also be integrated with other security devices such as cameras, alarms, and access control systems to provide a comprehensive security solution.</p><p>The global fiber optic perimeter security system market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
