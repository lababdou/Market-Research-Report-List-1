<p><strong>The Industrial Grade Zinc Sulfate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate</li><li>Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial grade zinc sulfate market comprises two main types: zinc sulfate monohydrate and zinc sulfate heptahydrate. Zinc sulfate monohydrate is a crystalline powder that contains one molecule of water per molecule of zinc sulfate. It is commonly used as a raw material in the manufacturing of fertilizers, animal feed supplements, and pharmaceuticals. Zinc sulfate heptahydrate, on the other hand, is a crystalline solid that contains seven molecules of water per molecule of zinc sulfate. It is primarily used in agriculture as a fertilizer and soil amendment to correct zinc deficiencies in crops.</p></p>
