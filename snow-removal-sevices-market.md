<p><p>The snow removal services market is primarily divided into two types: snow plowing and deicing. Snow plowing involves the use of specialized equipment to clear snow from roads, driveways, and parking lots. It typically involves pushing the snow to the side or into a designated area. On the other hand, deicing involves the application of chemicals or salt to melt existing ice and prevent further ice formation. Deicing is commonly used on roads, walkways, and other surfaces to improve traction and prevent accidents during icy conditions. Both services are crucial for maintaining safe and accessible environments during snowy and icy weather.</p></p>
