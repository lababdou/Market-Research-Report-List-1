<p><ul><li>Bag Dust Collector</li><li>Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP)</li><li>Electrostatic-Bag Precipitator (EBP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Plant Dust Collector Market consists of three main types: Bag Dust Collector, Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP), and Electrostatic-Bag Precipitator (EBP). A Bag Dust Collector is a system that collects dust particles on fabric filter bags using a reverse flow of air. An Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP) utilizes an electrical charge to remove dust particles from the air. Lastly, an Electrostatic-Bag Precipitator (EBP) combines the principles of both Bag Dust Collectors and ESPs, ensuring efficient dust removal. These technologies are commonly used in power plants to maintain clean and safe environments by reducing emissions.</p></p>
