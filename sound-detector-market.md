<p><p>The sound detector market consists of two main types: high frequency and low frequency. High frequency sound detectors are designed to detect and analyze sounds that have a high pitch or frequency, typically above 20,000 Hz. They are commonly used in industries such as security, animal behavior research, and ultrasonic imaging. On the other hand, low frequency sound detectors are specialized in capturing and interpreting sounds with a lower pitch or frequency, typically below 20 Hz. These detectors find applications in fields like earthquake monitoring, oceanography, and industrial noise control.</p></p>
