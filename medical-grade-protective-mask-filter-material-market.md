<p><p>The Medical Grade Protective Mask Filter Material Market includes two major types of filter materials: PP Nonwoven and PTFE Nonwoven. PP Nonwoven refers to a type of fabric made from polypropylene fibers, known for its high breathability and filtration capabilities. PTFE Nonwoven, on the other hand, is a fabric made from polytetrafluoroethylene, which offers excellent air permeability and filtration efficiency. Both materials are commonly used in manufacturing medical grade protective masks, providing essential filtration properties to protect against harmful particles and viruses.</p></p>
