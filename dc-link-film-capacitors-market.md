<p><p>DC link film capacitors are used in electrical and electronic systems for energy storage and voltage regulation purposes. There are two main types of DC link film capacitors: aluminum case package capacitors and plastic case package capacitors. Aluminum case package capacitors offer excellent thermal performance and high reliability, making them suitable for demanding applications. On the other hand, plastic case package capacitors are lightweight, cost-effective, and provide good self-healing properties, making them popular in less demanding applications. Both types contribute to efficient and reliable operation of DC link systems.</p></p>
