<p><p>Socket weld fittings refer to a category of pipe fittings used for connecting pipes in a piping system. These fittings come in various types, including elbows, tees, crosses, couplings, and others. Elbows are used to change the direction of the piping system, tees enable branching of the pipes, crosses allow four-way connections, and couplings help in connecting pipes of the same diameter. The "others" category includes different fittings like reducers, caps, and plugs, among others. All these fittings are designed to securely join pipes together, ensuring smooth flow of fluids or gases.</p></p>
