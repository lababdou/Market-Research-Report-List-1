<p><p>Pseudo Satellites are unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) designed to operate like satellites. There are two types of Pseudo Satellite markets: High-altitude Pseudo Satellites and Ground Pseudo Satellites. High-altitude Pseudo Satellites are UAVs that fly at extremely high altitudes, around 20-50 km above the Earth's surface, and can stay airborne for long durations, offering continuous monitoring capabilities for various applications such as communication, surveillance, and weather forecasting. Ground Pseudo Satellites, on the other hand, are UAVs that are tethered to the ground, providing similar functionalities but with restricted movement due to the tether. Both types have their unique advantages and utilization based on specific requirements.</p></p>
