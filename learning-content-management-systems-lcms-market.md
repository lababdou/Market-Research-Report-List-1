<p><p>Learning Content Management Systems (LCMS) are software applications used by organizations to create, manage, and deliver educational content. There are two main market types for LCMS: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise LCMS requires organizations to install and maintain the software on their own servers, providing more control over the system but requiring higher upfront costs. Cloud-Based LCMS, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering lower costs, scalability, and ease of access from any location.</p></p>
