<p><ul><li>Linear Cross Belt Sorting</li><li>Ring Cross Belt Sorting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crossbelt sorters are automated systems used in warehouses and distribution centers to efficiently sort and redirect items. There are two main types: linear crossbelt sorting and ring crossbelt sorting. Linear crossbelt sorting involves a straight conveyor belt where items are scanned and sorted into designated chutes along the belt. Ring crossbelt sorting consists of a circular conveyor belt where items are scanned and sorted into chutes positioned around the ring. Both types improve sorting speed and accuracy, enhancing overall productivity in logistics operations.</p></p>
