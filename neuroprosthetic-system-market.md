<p><strong>The Neuroprosthetic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Output Neural Prosthetics</li><li>Input Neural Prosthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Neuroprosthetic System market includes two types: Output Neural Prosthetics and Input Neural Prosthetics. Output Neural Prosthetics are devices that convert neural signals from the brain into actions, such as controlling prosthetic limbs or restoring motor functions. On the other hand, Input Neural Prosthetics are devices that enable the brain to receive sensory feedback, allowing individuals to perceive or feel sensations through the prosthetic limb. Both types of neuroprosthetics contribute to enhancing the quality of life and restoring physical function for individuals with neurological conditions or disabilities.</p></p>
